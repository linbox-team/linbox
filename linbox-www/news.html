8 July 2005 The first non-beta release, LinBox 1.0, has arrived!

LinBox 1.0 is our first "stable" release. The solutions section has undergone a major revision and extension with the purpose of making the linear algebra functions easily invoked. Also the examples directory contains illustrative uses of each function.

To obtain a copy, proceed to the download section. See release announcement.
